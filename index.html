Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will my donation help a family?

Your donation will be put to work within 72-96 hours. We have a streamlined process to identify families in need, verify their circumstances, and deliver immediate financial support for their most pressing needs.

Why focus specifically on single-parent families?

Single-parent households face disproportionate financial challenges. With only one income, no backup support at home, and the full weight of childcare and household responsibilities, these families are particularly vulnerable to financial shocks. A minor emergency can quickly spiral into a major crisis.

How do you ensure the money goes to families who need it?

We have a careful vetting process that includes documentation review, direct communication with families, and verification of their circumstances. We work with local community partners who help us identify families facing genuine hardship.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! liveLIVE Foundation is a registered nonprofit organization. You will receive a donation receipt for tax purposes. Please consult with your tax advisor regarding deductibility.

Can I donate if I'm not in the United States?

Absolutely! We welcome support from donors worldwide. Our payment processor accepts international donations.

What if you exceed the $50,000 goal?

If we're fortunate enough to exceed our goal, every additional dollar will be used to help more single-parent families beyond our initial target of 100. There's no shortage of families who could benefit from this support.
