Where Your Holiday Gift Goes

liveLIVE Foundation focuses on fast, practical support for single-parent households and veterans experiencing a temporary crisis. We work with trusted community partners to verify needs and get assistance out quickly.

Immediate Need

Emergency grocery cards, diapers, formula, and basic household essentials delivered within days so no child goes to bed hungry while a parent is trying to catch up.

Questions You Might Be Asking

How do you choose which families receive support?

We work with community partners, case managers, and direct referrals to identify single-parent households with an immediate, verifiable need. We prioritize situations where a short-term boost can prevent long-term crisis.

What does “90% goes directly to families” mean?

For this campaign, 90% of all donations are reserved strictly for direct assistance such as rent, utilities, food, transportation, and emergency needs. Only 10% is allocated to essential processing and administrative costs.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

liveLIVE Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records via email.
